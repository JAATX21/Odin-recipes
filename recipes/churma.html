Churma

Description

Churma is a dish of india which s favrout of every indian children.\ it consist of very healthy combination of ghee, dry fruits, wheat. Churma also works as instant energy source.

Ingredients

  • Wheat
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Ghee
  • Dry fruits
  • Sugar
  1. To blanch the almonds and pistachios, soak them in enough hot water for 15-20 minutes
  2. After that discard the water and peel the skin off. Now slice them using sharp knife
  3. Break them using your hand and add into the grinder jar
  4. Grind into coarse powder
  5. Remove it to a bowl
  6. Now add melted warm ghee
  7. Mix well till everything is incorporated well. If it looks dry then you can add more ghee.
  8. Now add sugar, cardamom powder and nuts.
  9. Mix well and it is ready to serve.
