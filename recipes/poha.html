Poha

Description

Poha is essentially flattened rice that has been parboiled, rolled, and dried into light, paper-thin flakes that act as a culinary sponge

Ingredients

  • Flattened Rice (Poha)
  • Tempering Aromatics
  • Vegetable Base
  • pinch of turmeric powde
  • squeeze of fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Flattened Rice Flakes: The foundation of the dish, these parboiled rice grains are rolled thin to create a light, airy base that softens with just a quick rinse.
  2. The Yellow Hue: Ground turmeric is the essential spice that gives the dish its signature bright gold color and a mild, earthy warmth.
  3. Crunchy Peanuts: Whole roasted or fried peanuts are mixed in to provide a nutty contrast to the soft, pillowy rice flakes.
  4. Aromatic Tempering: A flavorful oil base infused with popping mustard seeds, fresh curry leaves, and sliced green chillies for a hit of heat.
  5. Texture Boosters: Sautéed onions (for sweetness) or small potato cubes (for heartiness) are folded in to add bulk and flavor.
  6. The Bright Finish: Freshly squeezed lemon juice and a handful of chopped coriander to provide a zesty, refreshing tang that cuts through the savory spices.
